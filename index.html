Our Story

It all started one fateful day, circa 2019, when Jeani and Bennett both decided to dabble in dating apps and recklessly swiped right. Bennett was captivated by her devotion to alpacas and Studio Ghibli, and Jeani was spellbound by his apparent care-free attitude and self-proclaimed musical talent. After a bowl of pho and a romantic walk through campus, they realized how incredibly cool each other were, and the rest was history.

After many years of studying, tending to cats, and playing board games together, Beani were ready to take things to the next level. All it took was an oddly specific fortune cookie that prophesied, "All your dreams will come true in December." That month, on a tranquil winter walk through one of their favorite parks, the couple sat atop a large boulder and reminisced about their many adventures. It was at this moment when Bennett got up on one knee and proposed — all without falling backwards or dropping the ring. Jeani held her breath, kept her composure, and said yes!
